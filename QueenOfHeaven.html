Queen of Heaven - Huang Zou's Portfolio

Introduction

Queen of Heaven is a 5-person group project, including me, finished in 2 weeks. This is a combination of card game and tower defense game. There are four different types of cards: bronze, silver, gold, and diamond. Bronze card is an unconsumable card which is used to spawn your ally troop on a selected lane. Other cards can be drawn by clicking the full charged pole. The probability decreases from silver to diamond.
