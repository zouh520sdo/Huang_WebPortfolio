Introduction

OdettaIce is a two-player puzzle solving game. Each player controls each sprite to collaborate with each other to solve puzzles or beat monsters.

The blue sprite is called Odetta. Odetta's ability is to produce ice cube which can be used to heal Ice or demage enemies.

The brighter sprite is called Ice who is better at fighting.
